Using the app

Initial setup

Connect your device via USB

If your device is plugged in via USB, just hit "I'm connected" and the ap will do the rest. Make sure that you have enabled USB debugging.

For a wireless connection

Connect your Android via USB, go to File>Make Wireless. The rest is magic. After you get the OK, unplug your Android device and you will have a wireless connection!

Moving around

Use 'File' to easily access all of the availible functions and interfaces. Hiddem features are something that I personally enjoy and hope you wil too ;)
